## RISKS
### Dependence on Key Vendors
The analysis showed that a large portion of procurement spending comes from a small number of vendors, particularly DIAGEO NORTH AMERICA INC. This could create a risk if there are supplier issues, price increases, or changes in product availability.
### High Inventory Investment
The business holds approximately $79.70M in inventory value. While inventory is needed to support sales, maintaining high inventory levels may increase storage costs and tie up capital.
### Product Concentration
Several products consistently appeared among the top products for procurement spending, sales revenue, and inventory value. This suggests the business may be relying heavily on a small group of products to drive performance.

---
## OPPORTUNITIES

### Build Stronger Vendor Relationships
Since a few vendors contribute significantly to procurement and sales performance, maintaining strong relationships with these suppliers may help improve product availability and purchasing efficiency.
### Improve Inventory Planning
The inventory analysis showed that some products have high inventory value while others have high stock quantities. Reviewing inventory levels regularly may help improve stock management and reduce excess inventory.
### Focus on High-Performing Products
Products such as Jack Daniels No. 7 Black Label, Tito's Handmade Vodka, and Grey Goose Vodka performed strongly across multiple areas of the analysis. Similar products may present opportunities for future growth.
